Launched in 2016, C++ Day is a community-driven event format by the Italian C++ Community, co-organized with external partners like companies and universities.

The *C++ Day 2025* was held in person on **October 25** in **Pavia**. This event was a joint effort between the *Italian C++ Community* and SEA Vision, who also generously hostied the event at their venue. The event was free to attend, ran for an entire day, and included coffee breaks and lunch.

We had 165 attendees!
